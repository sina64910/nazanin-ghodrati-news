Iranian Woman Found Dead in Vancouver

Posted: Jan 14, 2023 8:23 AM PT | Last Updated: Jan 14, 2023

Vancouver police say the body of a 26-year-old Iranian woman, Nazanin Ghodrati, was found early Saturday morning near Granville Street.

Ghodrati had recently moved to Canada to pursue graduate studies in Clinical Biochemistry at the University of British Columbia (UBC). According to authorities, she had been living in a rental apartment in Vancouver's West Side for the past eight months.

Investigators have deemed the death "suspicious" and have taken a 32-year-old male, known to the victim, in for questioning. Police are asking anyone with information or surveillance footage from the area to contact them at 604-717-2500.

The Iranian-Canadian community has expressed shock over the incident and is calling for a thorough investigation.
